Singapore Cereal Prawns

  • Time: 20 minutes

  • Serves: 4 people

Ingredients Quantity
plain (all-purpose) flour 2 ½ tbsp
cornflour (cornstarch) 2 ½ tbsp
egg 1
original Nestum cereal 1 cup
caster (superfine) sugar 3 tsp
milk powder 1 tbsp
chicken stock 1 cube
vegetable oil for shallow frying
peeled king prawns, deveined 600g (1lb 5oz)
butter 100g (3.5 oz)
